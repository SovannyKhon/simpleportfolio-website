Culture Party

A cultural event serves as an enjoyable opportunity to introduce Khmer tradition to a new generation. This celebration celebrates many Khmer traditional festivals, including the Khmer New Year, to preserve and promote the rich cultural tapestry. Participants are invited to dress in traditional Khmer clothes, eat real Khmer food, perform traditional dances, and sing Khmer songs. The celebration is intended not just to entertain, but also to instill pride in and a connection to one's Khmer heritage. Attendees contribute to the preservation and restoration of Khmer traditions by participating in these cultural events, ensuring that they keep and resonate with future generations.
